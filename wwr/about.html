History

Our journey began in the early 1980s, with a passion for exploring the wild and a dedication to sharing the thrill of white water rafting with others. Over the decades, we have grown from a small group of enthusiasts into a renowned company, known for our expertise and commitment to safety.

From the roaring rapids of the Colorado River to the serene streams of the Smokey River, we have guided thousands of adventurers through some of the most beautiful and challenging waterways in the country. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience for all our guests.

Today, White Water Rafting continues to lead the industry, setting the standard for excellence and adventure. Join us and become part of our storied history!
